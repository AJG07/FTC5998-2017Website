Meet Phineas

Mecanum Wheel Drive Train

By having a mecanum wheel drive train, we are able to move omni-directional, or in all directions.

Two Fly Wheel Shooter

By having a two fly wheel shooter, we are able to achieve a balance of speed and accuracty.

Zip Tie Intake

Using a zip tie intake allows us to pickup balls in different situations with speed and agility, and store multiple balls to shoot at once.

Aluminum Extrusion (8020) Lift

Using a lift made out of Aluminum Extrusions allows us to raise and lower the lift quickly, and easily modify the lift with ease.

Linear Actuator Button Pushers

Linear actuator button pushers allow us to be very close to the wall in Autonomous to detect the color, and press the button accurately and effectively with ease.

Baltic Birch and 8020 Construction

Using baltic birch and 8020 as construction materials allow us to create a modular design, in which we can switch, modify, and fix certain parts of the robot with ease.
